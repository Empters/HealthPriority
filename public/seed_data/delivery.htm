1. You place your order with us

 

2.  On the next day your order is processed in our facility.

 

3. Shipping and Delivery time takes average between 5 and 6 work days.

The shipping charges are 4 GBP for orders below 50 GBP. Orders above 50 GBP are shipped for Free.

 

We ship all items within 24-hours of receiving your order. (Orders received after the last shipment on Fridays and on Public Holidays, are shipped on the next available business day following receipt of your order). We use BulPost, European Airmail Services (Priority Class), DHL and Royal Mail. You will be notified by us when your order has been shipped and informed of estimated delivery dates for your order. 

NOTE: healthpriority.co.uk do not profit from shipping charges. We charge you only the actual cost of packaging & shipping. Purchases are packed in such a way as to protect them from damage during transit from overseas, without adding additional weight which would increase air mail postage costs.
