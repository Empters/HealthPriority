HealthPriority.co.uk is a small, independent UK family business based in mainland Europe, from where we source and dispatch all our products. We also have a UK customer returns address for customer convenience.. We ship all our orders by Priority Air Mail Services from mainland Europe, within 24-hours of receipt of your order. (Orders placed after the last shipment on Fridays and on Public Holidays, are dispatched on the first available business day following receipt of your order). In most cases, orders  take between 5 & 7 business days to arrive at your address. ( Although many customers have reported faster delivery times than this). We keep our overheads to a minimum, to ensure we can always offer you excellent prices.

We sell a range of health, beauty & personal care products but have a particular interest in natural, environmentally-friendly products with 'eco-credentials', and which are not tested on animals. We research and select our merchandise with care, offering for sale high quality products at affordable prices. Our items are sourced from official distributers, suppliers and approved retailers. Where possible, we support small, local enterprises and initiatives, who are trying to operate businesses in a difficult ecomonic climate. The products we offer for sale are made by companies with a reputation for high quality & innovation, who are well-known, popular and trusted in mainland Europe. Many of these products are not (as yet) available in UK shops. Their countries of manufacture include Bulgaria, France, Poland, Italy and Germany. All our products conform to stringent E.U. regulations and standards.

We inspect each product before dispatching to customers, to ensure all seals are intact and all products are within date. As a matter of policy, we keep only small quantities of products on our premises. This is to ensure continuous turnover and frequent renewal of the products we offer for sale, so that you receive the latest version with a suffficiently long ''sell-by'' date. We re-stock at least  twice-weekly, more often when needed.

 

Products are stored on our premises in optimum conditions & temperatures and we operate a strictly non-smoking working environment, in accordance with EU Member States Legislation &  W.H.O recommendations.

We are aware that larger companies are able to offer free or subsidised postage and faster delivery, but we hope that you will agree that ourconsistantly fair & affordable prices and our attention to detail to ensure your products are protected during transit, make it worth your while waiting a few days longer for your order to arrive. 

We aim to offer you excellent customer service. We will send you an order confirmation email, inform you when your order has been dispatched and tell you the estimated delivery dates. Should you have questions or concerns about an order, please email at healthpriority@gmail.com. We will always try to resolve any problems quickly and to your satisfaction.

We hope you will try our products for your Health, Beauty & Personal Care.
