Q: How do HealthPriority.co.uk ensure shipments arrive intact? 
A: We do everything in our power to ensure your purchases reach you intact. All HealthPriority.co.uk shipments are carefully packed to protect the products during transit from overseas and to ensure safe delivery. All shipments are send by Priority Airmail Service to the UK. Our European airmail shippers have an excellent track record. 

Q: What happens once my order reaches the UK?
A: In the UK deliveries are made by Royal Mail. If there is no-one at your delivery address to receive your package, Royal Mail will leave a card informing you of this and telling you where you should collect it. If it is not collected within the time specified by Royal Mail, they will return the package to our UK address and we will inform you of this.
